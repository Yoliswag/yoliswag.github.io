About Me!

A little insight into my mind...

Hi, I'm Yoli!

I'm an artist inspired by the vibrant colors and eclectic styles of the 90s. My work is a mix of nostalgia, pop culture, and my own unique perspective on the world. From neon colors to grunge aesthetics, I love to explore different mediums and techniques to bring my ideas to life.

When I'm not creating art, you can find me listening to music, watching old cartoons, or exploring new ways to express myself. Welcome to my world!
